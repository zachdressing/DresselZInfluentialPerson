The reason that I chose Kyle Hill as my inspirational figure is because he embodies exactly what I want to be and be able to do. Hes genuine, funny, and knowledgeable, and he shares his knowledge with others in a way that provides new information to both the informed and the uninformed. On top of that, he has seemingly met and been able to hold positions near almost all of my childhood heroes, such as Bill Nye, Adam Savage, and Arin Hanson. He shares similar interests with me, specifically Magic the Gathering, and is known as a prominent figure in the hobby. On top of all of that, he has damn good hair.
